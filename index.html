Taxation section

Death and taxes are the only things that are inevitable in life.With the changing dynamics in tax laws in kenya, it makes tax compliance extreamly difficult. We work with you to determine the kind of tax obligation that is appropriate for you as a company or individual. We prepare accurate tax returns on monthly or annual basis to minimize business risk
The current tax in kenya are; Corporation Tax, PAYE, VAT, withholding tax, Advanced tax, residential rental income and digital service tax.

Our tax services include;

  • Acting as Tax agents for submission of tax returns (Corporation Tax, PAYE, VAT, withholding tax, Advanced tax, residential rental income and digital service tax.)
  • Responding to routine queries from Kenya Revenue Authority
  • Processing Personal Identification Numbers
  • Assisting in acquiring tax excemption status for qualifying clients
  • Personal Identification Number retrieval
  • Changing emails linked to Personal Identification Number
  • Amending Personal Identification Number details such as obligations
  • Application for Tax Compliance Certificate
